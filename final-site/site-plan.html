A Musical Site Plan

Austin Hatch

WDD 131-Section 2

Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to give people a chance to experience some of the best hits in some of my favorite plays. I want them to be able to press a button and hear some of the best hits from musicals, as well as get a brief synopsis and why they should check it out.

Audience

I think this would be awesome for people that love musical theatre, but it's more designed for people that haven't been exposed to it very much. Ideally people that would be open but don't know where to start.

Dynamic elements

I would have buttons that let you play specific songs over an audio channel, so they can hear the songs. I would also (ideally) be able to have a search menu and maybe even a 'random play' button. But we'll see how much is actually reasonable for me to accomplish.

Content

Home page

This is a list of all the images I might use in this just to have lots of things, I might even use them as a background for certain things.
I also think I'm going to have a description of what the website is for, links to maybe some website for tickets to these shows, and a welcome screen.

Musical Experience

This is a page where I will probably have a 'random play' generator and a link to an mp3 where you could listen to different hit songs from the play. It would also have a substantial synopsis of the play and maybe even have a card that can flip that would have a 'Spoiler' section so you could get more details if you didn't think you'd watch it.

Index of Musicals

This is the page where I would think about having a search bar and a list of plays you could look through. So you could find the play you want here and get details on the synopsis of each play and things.
